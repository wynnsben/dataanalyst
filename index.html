Recommendations

What the Data Suggests
Recommendation 01

Bundle Data with Wallet Rewards

Mobile Wallet and Mobile Data together account for over 40% of primary usage. Providers could pair generous data allowances with wallet cashback or load incentives — directly targeting the two most common behaviors in the dataset.

Recommendation 02

Engage the Neutral Segment Early

The 17.5% Neutral respondents are not dissatisfied — but they're not loyal either. Targeted retention efforts here, before a competitor's offer arrives, would cost far less than winning them back later.
